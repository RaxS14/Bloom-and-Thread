Our Role as Supplier

Our Materials & Suppliers — At Bloom And Threads, every piece begins with carefully chosen materials. We source our yarns, clips, and handles from trusted Shopee suppliers known for their quality and reliability.

Each thread and accessory is handpicked to match our standards of softness, color harmony, and long-lasting beauty. We make sure that every item — from hair clips to crochet tops, tumbler handles, and others is made with care and creativity.

By working with small and honest suppliers, we support local businesses while crafting handmade products that bring comfort, charm, and a personal touch to every customer.
