Privacy Policy

At Bloom & Thread, we value your privacy. Any personal information you provide, such as your name, contact number, and delivery address, will only be used to process and deliver your orders. We do not share, sell, or trade your information with third parties. All payment details, such as GCash transactions, remain private and secure.

Terms & Conditions

  • All crochet products are handmade, so slight variations in size, color, and design may occur.
  • Prices are subject to change without prior notice.
  • Orders are confirmed only after full or partial payment is received (depending on agreement).
  • Customers are responsible for providing correct delivery details.
  • We reserve the right to decline or cancel orders if necessary (e.g., incomplete details, unavailable materials).

Refund & Return Policy

  • Due to the handmade nature of our items, all sales are final.
  • Refunds or returns are only accepted if the product delivered is damaged or incorrect.
  • To request a return/refund, please contact us within 3 days of receiving your order with proof (photo/video).
  • Items must be unused and in their original condition.
  • For approved refunds, payment will be returned through GCash or the original payment method within 7–10 business days.

Supplier Information

Our raw materials for all crochet products are sourced from Shopee, ensuring high-quality fabrics, yarns, and accessories for every creation.
